Breakfast Burritos

Breakfast burritos are a big thing everywhere and with everyone

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Chorizo
  • Flour tortilla
  • Salt
  • Choice of salsa

steps

  1. In a pan thats hot, cook the chorizo for 5 minutes
  2. once the chorizo is fully cooked throw in your 2 whisked eggs.
  3. heat your four tortilla
  4. add salsa and wrap
